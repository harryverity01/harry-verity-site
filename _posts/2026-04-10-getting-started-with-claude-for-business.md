# Getting Started with Claude for Business Workflows

Most companies I talk to aren't looking for a revolution. They're looking for fewer hours spent on the stuff that shouldn't take hours in the first place.

Here's where I'd start.

## Look at the repetitive writing first

If your team writes similar emails, reports, or briefs on a regular basis, that's the lowest-hanging fruit. Claude is genuinely excellent at taking a rough brief and producing something polished — and once you've set up a good system prompt, the quality is remarkably consistent.

## Don't try to automate everything at once

Pick one workflow. The one that wastes the most collective time. Get that working smoothly before you move on. The companies that try to transform everything overnight usually end up transforming nothing.

## Start with the people who are already curious

There's always someone on the team who's been quietly playing with ChatGPT or Claude on their own. Start there. Let them be the internal champion, and the rest of the team will follow once they see the results.
